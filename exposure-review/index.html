The incident is rarely the exposure.
The exposure is usually procedural and already moving.

Operational Exposure Review is a fixed-scope written review for procurement, logistics, shipping and cross-border commercial arrangements already under operational movement.

The review identifies where delivery, documentation, payment, routing, notice, inspection or escalation conditions may degrade the recoverable position before the next operational decision is taken.

Scope

What is reviewed

Procedural exposure

Notice sequence, documentation timing, inspection position, handover steps and escalation routing.

Operational movement

Routing, delivery windows, customs, clearance, supplier coordination and third-party dependency.

Recoverable position

Whether the current sequence preserves or weakens the commercial position under delay, disruption or dispute pressure.

Recurrence risk

Patterns likely to repeat across similar purchase orders, shipments, releases, supplier terms or logistics arrangements.

Available review

Fixed-scope written exposure note

Designed for operations, procurement, logistics and commercial teams handling live arrangements where the next step may affect recoverability, payment, delivery, escalation or loss position.
