Use case

When this is useful

Use this when a delivery, shipment, supplier term, payment trigger, documentation sequence, inspection step, customs issue, notice chain or escalation path is already active and the next operational decision may affect recoverability.

What is reviewed
Procedural exposure

Notice sequence, documentation timing, inspection position, handover steps and escalation routing.

Operational movement

Routing, delivery windows, customs, clearance, supplier coordination and third-party dependency.

Recoverable position

Whether the current sequence preserves or weakens the commercial position under delay, disruption or dispute pressure.

Recurring pattern

Exposure likely to repeat across similar purchase orders, shipments, releases, supplier terms or logistics arrangements.
