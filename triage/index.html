Contract Operations Risk Triage is a fixed-scope written review for procurement, logistics, shipping and cross-border commercial agreements before signature, purchase order, supplier commitment, revised terms acceptance or operational release.

The review identifies where an RFQ, purchase order, supplier agreement, logistics arrangement or execution-sensitive contract may fail in practice through timing gaps, sequencing exposure, documentation dependency, delivery risk, notice issues, payment triggers, demurrage exposure and operational bottlenecks.

Scope

What is reviewed

Timing dependency

Delivery obligations, sequencing exposure, notice windows and procedural compression before execution begins.

Documentation dependency

Payment triggers, release conditions, inspection dependencies and documentary recovery gaps.

Operational continuity

Customs, clearance, third-party dependency, supplier coordination and logistics bottlenecks.

Commercial exposure

Demurrage, delay, disruption, allocation movement and loss of recoverable position under operational stress.
